2. tile/DnsFilterTileService.kt olustur:
   - TileService() extend et
   - private val serviceScope = CoroutineScope(SupervisorJob() + Dispatchers.IO)
   - Token ve URL icin: EncryptedSharedPreferences'tan token oku (TokenManager ile ayni dosya adi "tonbil_secure_prefs" kullan), DataStore'dan son bilinen URL oku
   - Ktor HttpClient dogrudan olustur (Koin kullanma — TileService bagimsiz surec olabilir)

   - onStartListening():
     * serviceScope.launch ile API'den guvenlik config'ini al (GET security/config)
     * SecurityConfigDto'dan dns_filtering alanlarini kontrol et (dnssec_enabled, tunneling_detection_enabled, doh_enabled, dga_detection_enabled — hepsi true ise aktif)
     * qsTile.state = STATE_ACTIVE veya STATE_INACTIVE
     * qsTile.label = "DNS Filtre"
     * qsTile.subtitle = "Acik" veya "Kapali"
     * qsTile.updateTile()
     * Hata durumunda STATE_UNAVAILABLE

   - onClick():
     * serviceScope.launch ile mevcut durumu tersine cevir
     * PATCH security/config ile 4 DNS alanini birden toggle et: SecurityConfigUpdateDto(dnssec_enabled=newState, tunneling_detection_enabled=newState, doh_enabled=newState, dga_detection_enabled=newState)
     * Basarili ise tile state guncelle
     * Hata durumunda orijinal state'e geri don

   - onDestroy(): serviceScope.cancel()
